This is a Visualization of the Centipede Game in Game Theory. There are two players switching turns and by continuing they increase the payoff but also the chance the other player quits getting a better payoff.

Based on Backwards Induction the best play for the first player is to quit on the first round, if they expects, the game won't continue till the end.
